# Flowitec — Engineering & Procurement Solutions Website

Official website for **Flowitec Group**, a leading engineering and procurement company operating across Africa (Ghana, Nigeria, Kenya, South Africa).

🌐 **Live Site:** [flowitec.com](https://flowitec.com)

---

## About the Project

Flowitec needed a professional, high-performance corporate website to showcase their industrial products, services, and Africa-wide operations. The site serves as the primary digital presence for the company across all four of their African offices.

### Key Features

- **Product Catalogue** — Browse pumps, valves, motors, control panels, and spare parts with detailed product pages
- **Industry Pages** — Dedicated pages for Mining, Agriculture, Petrochemical, Food & Beverage, and more
- **Applications Section** — Solutions mapped to specific use cases (clean water, slurry, mine water, fuel, etc.)
- **Services Pages** — Maintenance, installation, diagnostics, training, and spare parts
- **Quote Request System** — Integrated contact and quote forms for client enquiries
- **Partner Showcase** — Displays global manufacturer partnerships (C.R.I., Franklin Electric, Bray, etc.)
- **Multi-office Contact Info** — Covers Ghana, Nigeria, Kenya, and South Africa offices
- **Responsive Design** — Fully optimised for mobile, tablet, and desktop
- **Performance Optimised** — Fast image loading with Next.js image optimisation
